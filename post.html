Ep 01 // Himachal Pradesh

Lost in the Snow

Location: Manali • Solang Valley | January 2026

00:15 / THE ARRIVAL

The First Glimpse

I woke up in the luxury sleeper bus just as the sun was hitting the peaks. The winding roads were terrifying, but look at this view. I checked into my wood-paneled cabin and the air was crisp, almost sweet. Manali is already magical.

02:45 / SOLANG

Whiteout at Solang Valley

We drove down to Solang Valley for some skiing. I have never seen snow this deep! The entire valley was painted white. I failed miserably at skiing, but the maggi point at the top made everything better.

06:10 / CAFE HOPPING

Old Manali Vibes

Spent the evening exploring the cafes in Old Manali. The mood changes completely after sunset. Fireplaces, hot chocolate, and some live acoustic music. This is exactly what I needed.
